## 3D Space Conventions

- Axis convention: Clockwise is positive
- 3D rotation: intrinsic, active, z-y-x order (Tait-Bryan angles)
- Coordinate system: Right-handed, Y as up, X as right, Z pointing out of screen
- Matrix & Vector: Column-major (OpenGL standard), use GLM
- Transform operation order: position -> rotation -> scale
